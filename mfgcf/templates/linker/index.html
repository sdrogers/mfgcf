Molecular Family analysis is an analysis of structurally related molecules based on their mass spectral fragmentation patterns. At the moment, we accept output from GNPS.

Gene Cluster Family analysis is an analysis of biosynthetic gene clusters that show similar gene cluster organization with a high degree of sequence similarity. At the moment, we accept output from BiGSCAPE.
